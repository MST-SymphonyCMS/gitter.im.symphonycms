In XML/XSLT, if you want to use entities other than the predefined entities (AFAIK: &amp;, &lt;, &gt;, &quot;, &apos;), you must explicitely declare them at the top of your stylesheet.

<!DOCTYPE xsl:stylesheet  [
    <!ENTITY nbsp   "&#160;">
    <!ENTITY euro   "&#8364;">
    <!ENTITY uuml   "ü">
    <!ENTITY ouml   "ö">
    <!ENTITY auml   "ä">
]>

I don't know, however, why the datasource produces such an output. I have no experience with the Search Index extension.
