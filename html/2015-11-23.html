Jonathan Mifsud

Nov 23 2015 13:33
From my understanding (and this is how I used it) all amounts are managed outside of the session context.
you basically want to store that the user has ‘1 tshirt’ in his cart. and maybe if you have colours that would be ‘1 blue tshirt’ then it’s when you render the page using a datasource that you would provide the amounts/totals.
You don’t want your amounts to be ‘adjustable’ by someone playing around with your session data.
