Jonathan Mifsud

it depends on your implementation.
If the person doesn’t accept cookies you’re not allowed to use them. The two options are to allow browsing and remove any tracking such as GA etc. or else tell the user they cannot use the site without cookies and send him back somewhere else. (first one preferred if you care about your users) - or else if you want to have cookies but not kickthem out put a one way notice that they agree with cookies and only give ‘ok’ as an option. not sure if that covers you legally
